National programs recruiting former feds

Civic Match (Work for America) ↗

Nonprofit matching former federal employees with state and local government roles nationwide. Nearly 200 placements to date — free for job seekers.

Minnesota — Federal Workers Hub ↗

Minnesota's dedicated landing page for federal workers moving to state service, with translated job categories.

NASPO State Procurement Initiative ↗

National program routing federal contracting and procurement talent (GS-1102 and adjacent) into state purchasing offices.
