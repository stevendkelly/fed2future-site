GS-1102 Contract Specialist: What It Translates To Outside the Federal Government

Series 1102 · Typical grades GS-7 to GS-14 · SOC 13-1023 · Typical federal pay $48K–$150K

In plain EnglishYou did federal acquisition end-to-end: solicitations, negotiations, awards, administration under the FAR — some of the most rigorous procurement training anywhere.
Private-sector titles to searchProcurement Manager · Sourcing Manager · Contracts Manager · Subcontracts Administrator

How to reframe this experience

  • ✓NASPO and states are actively recruiting federal procurement talent — this is a seller's market.
  • ✓FAR fluency → corporate procurement rigor; say “strategic sourcing.”
  • ✓Defense contractors need your exact skills on the other side of the table (subcontracts, capture).
