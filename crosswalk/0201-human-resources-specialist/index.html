GS-0201 Human Resources Specialist: What It Translates To Outside the Federal Government

Series 0201 · Typical grades GS-7 to GS-13 · SOC 13-1071 · Typical federal pay $48K–$125K

In plain EnglishYou handled staffing, classification, employee relations, or benefits under Title 5 — some of the most process-rigorous HR work anywhere.
Private-sector titles to searchHR Business Partner · Talent Acquisition Specialist · HR Generalist

How to reframe this experience

  • ✓Translate Title 5 terms: “staffing” → recruiting/talent acquisition; “classification” → job architecture/compensation.
  • ✓Emphasize compliance depth — states and regulated industries prize it.
  • ✓Certifications (SHRM-CP/PHR) close the private-sector gap fast.
