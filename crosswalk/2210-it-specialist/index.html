GS-2210 IT Specialist (2210): What It Translates To Outside the Federal Government

Series 2210 · Typical grades GS-9 to GS-14 · SOC 15-1211 / 15-1212 · Typical federal pay $60K–$155K

In plain EnglishYou ran federal IT: sysadmin, network, cybersecurity, applications, data, or IT project management — likely with FISMA/RMF compliance baked in.
Private-sector titles to searchSystems Administrator · Cybersecurity Analyst · Cloud Engineer · IT Project Manager

How to reframe this experience

  • ✓Certifications carry the resume: Security+, CISSP, AWS/Azure — most 2210s already qualify for one exam away.
  • ✓RMF/FISMA → “GRC and compliance frameworks (NIST 800-53)” — enterprise security teams need this.
  • ✓Clearance + cyber is the single hottest combination in the market.
