GS-0905 General Attorney: What It Translates To Outside the Federal Government

Series 0905 · Typical grades GS-11 to GS-15 · SOC 23-1011 · Typical federal pay $80K–$190K

In plain EnglishYou practiced law for the government: litigation, regulatory work, contracts, enforcement, or agency counsel.
Private-sector titles to searchCounsel / Senior Counsel · Compliance Officer · Regulatory Affairs Director

How to reframe this experience

  • ✓Your regulatory specialty is your brand — market the subject matter, not “government lawyer.”
  • ✓State AG offices and agency counsel roles are the closest analog.
  • ✓In-house compliance roles pay well for former regulators; check bar admission by state.
