GS-0501 Financial Administration Specialist: What It Translates To Outside the Federal Government

Series 0501 · Typical grades GS-7 to GS-13 · SOC 13-2099 · Typical federal pay $48K–$125K

In plain EnglishYou managed financial operations: funds control, reporting, reconciliation, financial systems — the practical side of federal money.
Private-sector titles to searchFinancial Analyst · Finance Operations Manager · Financial Reporting Specialist

How to reframe this experience

  • ✓Federal appropriations discipline reads as strong internal controls.
  • ✓Name systems (Momentum, Oracle Federal, SAP) — ERP experience is the keyword.
  • ✓Reconciliation and funds control = accounting operations roles.
