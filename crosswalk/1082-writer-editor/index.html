GS-1082 Writer-Editor: What It Translates To Outside the Federal Government

Series 1082 · Typical grades GS-7 to GS-12 · SOC 27-3042 · Typical federal pay $48K–$105K

In plain EnglishYou wrote and edited for the government: reports, web content, regulations, correspondence, publications.
Private-sector titles to searchContent Manager · Technical Writer · Communications Specialist

How to reframe this experience

  • ✓Regulatory and plain-language writing maps to technical writing (which pays better).
  • ✓Portfolio beats resume in this field — assemble one before applying.
  • ✓Healthcare, finance, and legal publishers value government-document fluency.
