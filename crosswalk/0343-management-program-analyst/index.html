GS-0343 Management & Program Analyst: What It Translates To Outside the Federal Government

Series 0343 · Typical grades GS-9 to GS-14 · SOC 13-1111 · Typical federal pay $55K–$150K

In plain EnglishYou analyzed how programs perform — data, process improvement, reporting, studies — and told leadership what to fix.
Private-sector titles to searchBusiness Analyst · Management Consultant · Operations Analyst · Process Improvement Manager

How to reframe this experience

  • ✓This is the most translatable series in government — you are a business analyst.
  • ✓Name your toolkit: Excel/data analysis, process mapping, Lean, dashboards.
  • ✓Convert findings into outcomes: “analysis cut processing time 30%.”
