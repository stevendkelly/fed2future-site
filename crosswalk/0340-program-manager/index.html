GS-0340 Program Manager: What It Translates To Outside the Federal Government

Series 0340 · Typical grades GS-13 to GS-15 · SOC 11-1021 · Typical federal pay $105K–$185K

In plain EnglishYou owned programs end-to-end: strategy, budget, staffing, stakeholders, and results — executive-level general management.
Private-sector titles to searchDirector of Operations · Senior Program Manager · General Manager

How to reframe this experience

  • ✓State the scope in numbers first: budget, headcount, population served.
  • ✓Federal “program” = private-sector P&L or business unit — say so.
  • ✓PMP certification instantly validates the title outside government.
