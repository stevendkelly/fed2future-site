GS-0511 Auditor: What It Translates To Outside the Federal Government

Series 0511 · Typical grades GS-9 to GS-14 · SOC 13-2011 · Typical federal pay $55K–$150K

In plain EnglishYou audited programs, grants, or financial statements — planning, fieldwork, findings, and reports under GAGAS (Yellow Book).
Private-sector titles to searchInternal Auditor · Senior Auditor · Compliance Auditor · Risk & Controls Analyst

How to reframe this experience

  • ✓Yellow Book experience maps directly to internal audit and SOX work.
  • ✓Emphasize risk assessment and controls testing vocabulary.
  • ✓CIA/CISA certifications accelerate private-sector credibility.
