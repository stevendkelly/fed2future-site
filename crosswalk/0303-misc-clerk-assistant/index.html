GS-0303 Miscellaneous Clerk & Assistant: What It Translates To Outside the Federal Government

Series 0303 · Typical grades GS-4 to GS-7 · SOC 43-9061 · Typical federal pay $36K–$58K

In plain EnglishYou were the operational backbone: records, correspondence, scheduling, data entry, front-line support for a federal office.
Private-sector titles to searchAdministrative Assistant · Office Coordinator · Operations Assistant

How to reframe this experience

  • ✓Lead with systems you mastered (records systems, scheduling, correspondence control).
  • ✓Emphasize volume and accuracy metrics.
  • ✓Highlight any lead/training responsibility.
