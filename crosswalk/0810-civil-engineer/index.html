GS-0810 Civil Engineer: What It Translates To Outside the Federal Government

Series 0810 · Typical grades GS-9 to GS-14 · SOC 17-2051 · Typical federal pay $70K–$155K

In plain EnglishYou designed, reviewed, or managed civil works: roads, water, structures, environmental projects for agencies like USACE, FHWA, or BuRec.
Private-sector titles to searchCivil Engineer · Project Manager (AEC) · Construction Manager

How to reframe this experience

  • ✓State DOTs are the largest civil engineering employers in most states — direct fit.
  • ✓PE license is the door-opener; federal design review counts toward it.
  • ✓AEC consulting firms actively recruit federal reviewers.
