GS-0962 Contact Representative: What It Translates To Outside the Federal Government

Series 0962 · Typical grades GS-5 to GS-8 · SOC 43-4051 · Typical federal pay $40K–$66K

In plain EnglishYou were the public's voice of the agency — SSA, IRS, VA call centers and field offices — resolving complex cases under strict rules.
Private-sector titles to searchCustomer Service Specialist · Claims Processor · Member Services Representative

How to reframe this experience

  • ✓You handled harder cases than most call centers ever see — say that plainly.
  • ✓Insurance, health plans, and banks prize regulated-environment CS experience.
  • ✓State benefit agencies (Medicaid, unemployment) are near-identical work.
