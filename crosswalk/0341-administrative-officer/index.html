GS-0341 Administrative Officer: What It Translates To Outside the Federal Government

Series 0341 · Typical grades GS-9 to GS-13 · SOC 11-3012 · Typical federal pay $55K–$125K

In plain EnglishYou ran the business side of a federal organization: budget execution, procurement coordination, space, HR liaison, logistics.
Private-sector titles to searchBusiness Operations Manager · Office Director · Chief of Staff (small org)

How to reframe this experience

  • ✓Position as multi-function operations management.
  • ✓Quantify the budget you executed and contracts you coordinated.
  • ✓“Everything officer” maps to ops manager roles in mid-size companies.
