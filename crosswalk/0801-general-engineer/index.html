GS-0801 General Engineer: What It Translates To Outside the Federal Government

Series 0801 · Typical grades GS-9 to GS-14 · SOC 17-2199 · Typical federal pay $70K–$155K

In plain EnglishYou did engineering work spanning disciplines: facilities, systems, projects, or technical program oversight.
Private-sector titles to searchProject Engineer · Engineering Program Manager · Facilities Engineer

How to reframe this experience

  • ✓PE licensure (or eligibility) is the single biggest lever — lead with it.
  • ✓Federal oversight experience maps to owner's-engineer and program management roles.
  • ✓Contractors serving your former agency want your acquisition fluency.
