GS-1811 Criminal Investigator (Special Agent): What It Translates To Outside the Federal Government

Series 1811 · Typical grades GS-9 to GS-13 + LEAP · SOC 33-3021 · Typical federal pay $65K–$145K

In plain EnglishYou conducted federal criminal investigations: case work, interviews, warrants, task forces, testimony — at FBI, HSI, IRS-CI, or similar.
Private-sector titles to searchCorporate Investigator · Fraud Investigator · Security Director · Compliance Investigator

How to reframe this experience

  • ✓Financial crimes experience → bank fraud/AML units pay very well.

  • ✓Corporate security and insurance SIU actively recruit 1811s.
