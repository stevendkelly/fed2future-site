GS-1550 Computer Scientist: What It Translates To Outside the Federal Government

Series 1550 · Typical grades GS-11 to GS-15 · SOC 15-1221 · Typical federal pay $80K–$185K

In plain EnglishYou did applied computing R&D: algorithms, modeling, scientific computing, AI/ML for defense, energy, or research agencies.
Private-sector titles to searchSoftware Engineer · Machine Learning Engineer · Research Scientist

How to reframe this experience

  • ✓Private tech pays 1.5–2x government for this skill set — aim high.
  • ✓Publish/demo a portfolio; industry hires on demonstrated code and models.
  • ✓National-lab and defense-research experience is a differentiator for AI-safety and defense-tech firms.
