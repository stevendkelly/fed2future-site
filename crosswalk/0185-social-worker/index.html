GS-0185 Social Worker (Federal): What It Translates To Outside the Federal Government

Series 0185 · Typical grades GS-9 to GS-12 · SOC 21-1022 · Typical federal pay $55K–$105K

In plain EnglishYou provided clinical or case-management social work, most often at VA: mental health, care coordination, crisis intervention, discharge planning.
Private-sector titles to searchClinical Social Worker (LCSW) · Case Management Director · Behavioral Health Manager

How to reframe this experience

  • ✓Your LCSW transfers with state licensure — start that paperwork first.
  • ✓VA complex-care experience is prized by health systems and hospices.
  • ✓Telehealth platforms hire LCSWs at competitive rates with full flexibility.
