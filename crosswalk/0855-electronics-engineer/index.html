GS-0855 Electronics Engineer: What It Translates To Outside the Federal Government

Series 0855 · Typical grades GS-9 to GS-14 · SOC 17-2072 · Typical federal pay $75K–$160K

In plain EnglishYou engineered electronic and RF systems: radar, communications, avionics, test — often for DoD, FAA, or NASA.
Private-sector titles to searchElectrical/Electronics Engineer · RF Engineer · Systems Engineer

How to reframe this experience

  • ✓Defense contractors are the natural first stop — clearance plus domain is a premium combo.
  • ✓Telecom, aerospace, and test/measurement firms speak your language.
  • ✓State roles cluster in transportation tech and public safety comms.
