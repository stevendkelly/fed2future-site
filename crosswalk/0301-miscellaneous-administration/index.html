GS-0301 Miscellaneous Administration & Program Specialist: What It Translates To Outside the Federal Government

Series 0301 · Typical grades GS-7 to GS-13 · SOC 11-1021 / 13-1199 · Typical federal pay $48K–$125K

In plain EnglishYou kept programs and offices running: coordinating projects, managing processes, handling special assignments that cross organizational lines.
Private-sector titles to searchProgram Coordinator · Operations Specialist · Project Coordinator

How to reframe this experience

  • ✓Translate program names into functions: “managed intake process serving 5,000 clients/year,” not office symbols.
  • ✓Quantify: budgets touched, people served, cycle times improved.
  • ✓Drop grade/series language entirely from the resume.
