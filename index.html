The problem nobody built for — until now

The federal government classifies your career into one of ~440 occupational series. Every state uses its own incompatible titles and pay bands. OPM's only crosswalk stops at statistical codes. So when 300,000 careers were disrupted, every single person had to decode 50 state systems alone. Fed2Future puts the whole map in one place.

✓ Your series, translated

Enter your GS series and get the closest state classifications, private-sector titles, and realistic salary ranges — in plain English, not HR-speak.

✓ Where the jobs actually are

States like Pennsylvania now credit federal experience as state experience by executive order. New York is filling ~7,000 vacancies. We track who's hiring feds and where to apply.

✓ The money side, covered

TSP rollovers, FERS decisions, severance taxes, salary negotiation — guidance from a CPA who's navigated career pivots at the highest level. Most career sites can't read your TSP statement. We can.
